Design

Graphic Design is not only a part of my lectures but also of my personal interests. Therefore I spend some time deigning something new and challenging my creativity.

Here are some projects where I was involved in the Graphic Design.

Lost and Found

In this project, the task was to design a website for lost and found items on the Hagenberg Campus. Initially, we collaborated in groups before proceeding to refine the design individually. I utilized Figma to create prototypes, which was an enriching experience as it introduced me to the workings of the program. This project served as my introduction to the creative process of design, allowing me to explore and refine my skills in this area.

Mon Éclat

Mon Éclat is a fictional business operating in the beauty industry, and I was tasked with designing its corporate identity (CI). This project provided a unique learning experience as I integrated the design process with strategic planning, effectively developing the entire business plan alongside the design and prototypes. This comprehensive approach allowed me to gain valuable insights into the interconnected aspects of branding, design, and business strategy.

Ethics Website

For this website project, I not only took part in the programming but also took charge of sourcing and incorporating 3D models. This presented a notable challenge as we lacked resources such as time and budget to procure our own or high-quality 3D models. My responsibility involved identifying suitable 3D models for each topic and converting them into the appropriate format to integrate seamlessly into our project.
